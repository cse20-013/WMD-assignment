Beijing Daxing International Airport

Get to Beijing and arrive in style at Beijing's new International airport

Located on the border of Beijing, this state of the art masterpiece now holds the record of being the largest single body terminal at 700 000suare meters. This airport took 4years to be built, constructions starting on the 26th of December 2014 and was completed on the 30th of June 2019 and was offically opened on the 26th of September 2019.
This $11.5billion project was designed by Zaha Hadid Architects.

It houses facilities like airport lounges, airport ambassadors, 5G Wi-Fi, ATMs, Baby facilites, custom declaration, customs prohibited goods, foreign exchange currency, easily rechable gates, eco-friendly airport, lost and found, medical rooms, parking, post services, smoking policy, special needs passengers facilites, tax refund services, temporary flight certificate, traditonal chinese gardens, travel insurance, gender-free bathrooms, VIP services, yoga rooms and retail services.
