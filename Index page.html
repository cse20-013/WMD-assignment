Welcome To Beijing

Ni Hao

That is a Chinese welcome remark which simply means "Hello" to welcome you to our page!!!!
We present you with the mother city, the capital of China which is also know as Peking the People's Republic of China.
It is one the world's most populous national capital city, with over 21 million residents within an administrative area of 16,410.5 km (6336 sq. mi.)
Located at the west coast of China ,Beijing lays at the northern tip of the China plain near the meeting point of the Xishan and Yanshan mountain ranges surely making the winter even longer and chiller.
